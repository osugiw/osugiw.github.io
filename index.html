About Me

Sugiarto graduated with a bachelor's degree in electrical engineering from Petra Christian University, Indonesia. Upon finishing his bachelor's degree, he worked as an embedded engineer at the biggest electronics manufacturer in Indonesia, namely Polytron Indonesia. After working for several years, he decided to pursue a master's in electrical & computer engineering at Rice University, United States, to deepen his skills and knowledge in the area of wearable technologies. In the future, he aspires to innovate technologies to support the elderly's daily lives.

His expertise includes embedded software programming, Internet of Things, and system integration that covers areas of home appliances and wearable technology applications. To deepen his skills, he continuously learn and experiments with the state-of-the-art technology. He also often works with his friends on some small-scale projects for clients from across countries.

His hobbies are brewing coffee, watching movies, cooking, culinary, exploring new places, and strolling around.
